infoWHAT IS LIVE RESIN?

Live resin is an extract made from fresh frozen plant material that has not been dried or cured. The flower buds are harvested and immediately put into a cryogenic process undergoing freezing temperatures. By omitting these steps, the plants' terpenoids and potency are preserved.

infoWHAT ARE LIQUID DIAMONDS?

Liquid diamonds are premium cannabis extracts that contain higher THC contents. Diamonds are a combination of THCA diamonds and live resin sauce. The concentrates get separated and then decarbed leaving a higher purity extract for a powerful and more intense experience.

infoWHAT IS LIVE ROSIN?

Live Rosin is a solventless cannabis concentrate made from fresh frozen plant material. The material gets emitted to subzero temperatures directly from the harvest then heat pressed to extract oil from the plant. Live Rosin keeps most of the trichomes of the product active, providing strong & potent user effects.

infoWHY QC BLENDS?

We have developed a unique oil blend that preserves the live plant terpenoids (flavor) & adds a higher THC % in our disposables. Our fresh frozen gets extracted one flavor at a time & undergoes 3 different processes for each extract known as Live Resin, Liquid Diamonds, & Live Rosin. Most companies create extracts from different strains, combine them & add artificial terpenes to make another flavor. This results in a loss of the natural terpene profiles & effects. Under our extraction method we don't allow our material to exceed certain temperatures; enabling us to retain all the natural profiles directly from the plant. We ensure that every flavor is truly preserved & our disposables have the highest thc % possible throughout our process.
